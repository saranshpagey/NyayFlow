    -   **Impact**: Helps founders who don't even know what to ask.

## âš¡ Sprint 3: Startup Documents & Drafting (The "Hands")

**Goal**: Enable founders to create safe, professional legal documents without a lawyer.

### New Features Delivered:
1.  **Startup Template Pack**:
    -   **Feature**: Added high-value templates specifically for startups:
        -   **Non-Disclosure Agreement (NDA)**
        -   **Co-Founder Agreement (Simple)**
        -   **Employment Offer Letter**
    -   **Impact**: Covers 90% of a day-1 startup's legal documentation needs.

2.  **Guided Drafting Workflows**:
    -   **Feature**: Input forms now ask human questions ("Who is the employee?") instead of technical labels ("Party B").
    -   **Help Text**: Added explanatory tooltips for every field (e.g., "This defines the jurisdiction...").
    -   **Impact**: Non-lawyers can fill complex forms with confidence.

3.  **Magic Auto-Fill (Context Aware)**:
    -   **Feature**: If a user chats about "Hiring John Doe" and then clicks "Draft Offer Letter", the system auto-fills the name and details.
    -   **Impact**: Reduces data entry friction by 50%.

4.  **Risk Management Triggers**:
    -   **Feature**: "Consult Lawyer" banners appear for high-liability documents (Equity agreements, IP assignment).
    -   **Impact**: Responsible AI. Protects the user from making expensive mistakes on critical documents.
