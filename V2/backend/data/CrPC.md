## **Overview**

The Code of Criminal Procedure (CrPC) is the principal legislation on the procedure for administration of substantive criminal law in India. This dataset provides a structured breakdown of the CrPC, including chapters, sections, and detailed descriptions.

## **Dataset Structure**

* File: (Replace with actual filename, e.g., crpc\_sections.csv)  
* Columns:  
  * Chapter: Chapter number  
  * Chapter\_name: Name of the chapter  
  * Section: Section number  
  * Section\_name: Title of the section  
  * Description: Full text of the section

## **Data Source**

The data is based on the official text of the Code of Criminal Procedure, as published by the Government of India. The dataset is organized for computational and research purposes.

## **Use Cases**

* Legal information retrieval and search  
* NLP and machine learning on legal texts  
* Legal education and reference  
* Comparative analysis with new criminal procedure codes
