SABINA, J.
Harjit Kaur and Deepak Attari-petitioners have filed this
petition under
Section 482
of the Code of Criminal Procedure for
issuance of directions to respondent Nos. 1 to 3 to protect their life
and liberty from the hands of respondent Nos.4 to 6.
Learned counsel for the petitioners has submitted that
the marriage of the petitioners was solemnized on 6.10.2008.
Photographs in this regard have been annexed as Annexure P-3.
The petitioners have moved a representation to Senior
Superintendent of Police, Nawanshahar and a copy of the same has
been annexed as Annexure P-4.
Accordingly, without expressing any opinion on the merits
of the case, Senior Superintendent of Police, Nawanshahar-
respondent No.2 is directed to look into the representation made by
Crl.Misc.No.M- 3289 of 2009 (O&M) -2-
the petitioners (Annexure P-4) and if required, necessary protection
be provided to them.
Petition stands disposed of in the above terms.
