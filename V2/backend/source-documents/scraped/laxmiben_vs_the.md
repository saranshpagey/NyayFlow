ORAL
ORDER
Mr.P.M.Lakhani,
learned advocate appearing for Mr.Dholakia, learned advocate
appearing on behalf of the petitioner, has stated at the bar that the
entire amount due and payable to the petitioner is paid and,
therefore, the grievance does not survive. Hence, he seeks permission
to withdraw the present petition as grievance does not survive.
Accordingly the present petition is dismissed as withdrawn as the
grievance does not survive. Rule is discharged. Ad-interim relief, if
any, stands vacated forthwith. No costs.
