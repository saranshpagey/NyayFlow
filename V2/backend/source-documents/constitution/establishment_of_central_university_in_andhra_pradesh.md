Article
371E
Establishment of Central University in Andhra Pradesh
Parliament may by law provide for the establishment of a University in the State of Andhra Pradesh.
Summary
Article 371E was not a part of the Constitution of India 1950. It was inserted by the
Constitution (Thirty-second Amendment) Act, 1973
, following which the University of Hyderabad was established in 1974.
