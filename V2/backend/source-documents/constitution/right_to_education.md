Article
21A
Right to education
The State shall provide free and compulsory education to all children of the age of six to fourteen years in such manner as the State may, by law, determine.
Summary
This provision was not included in the Constitution of India 1950. It was inserted by the
Constitution (Eighty-sixth Amendment) Act, 2010
.
