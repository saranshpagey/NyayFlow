Article
144
Civil and judicial authorities to act in aid of the Supreme Court
All authorities, civil and judicial, in the territory of India shall act in aid of the Supreme Court.
Version 1
Article 120, Draft Constitution of India 1948
All authorities, civil and judicial, in the territory of India shall act in aid of the Supreme Court.
Version 2
Article 144, Constitution of India 1950
All authorities, civil and judicial, in the territory of India shall act in aid of the Supreme Court.
Summary
Draft Article 120 (Article 144) was debated in the Constituent Assembly on
27th May 1949
. It stipulated that all civil authorities must act in aid of the Supreme Court.
The Draft Article was accepted without debate and
adopted
by the Assembly on 27th May 1949.
